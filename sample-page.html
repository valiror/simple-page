About Us

Glassic is India's only private label brand that's disrupting the eyewear industry. The eyewear industry is skewed with almost no quality/brand players in the low to medium end of the market & all brands crowding in the medium to high end of the market.

Glassic aims to unify the affordable market by creating a quality brand that people can trust. By designing & manufacturing in house & selling our eyewear online, we keep a close check on our quality and, at the same time, provide branded quality at super affordable prices.

We're hardcore industry guys with strong domain expertise. With quality at the heart of everything we do, we're looking to make glasses fun and create a cool brand & company out of India that creates value for consumers in ways they could never imagine.
